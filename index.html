About Abhay Tirtha

A Sacred Vision Coming to Life

Abhay Tirtha represents more than just a temple - it embodies the eternal teachings of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada and the timeless wisdom of Krishna consciousness. Located in the vibrant city of Newtown, Kolkata, this divine project will serve as a spiritual sanctuary for thousands of devotees.

The temple will feature magnificent architecture inspired by traditional Vedic design, spacious prayer halls for congregational worship, educational facilities for spiritual learning, and serene gardens for meditation and reflection. Every brick laid with devotion will contribute to creating a sacred space where souls can reconnect with their divine nature.

Divine Members

Join the Divine Instruments of Lord Krishna

Divine members represent the sacred instruments through which Lord Krishna performs His divine pastimes. Each tier is named after the divine weapons and symbols of Lord Vishnu, representing strength, purity, protection, and victory in spiritual life.

Symbol of purity and spiritual awakening
